Rules and Regulations

  • The student should apply online using their institute e-mail IDs only. Personal IDs and unauthenticated users will not be allowed to apply for hostel room allotment.
  • The students of a particular batch may only apply in the hostel allotted to them as decided by the administration.
  • The student may only fill the room preference from the range of rooms allotted to the particular batch the student belongs in.
  • The rooms will be allotted strictly on a First Come First Serve basis.
  • No student is allotted a room until his Mess fees has been paid. The Mess Fees receipt reference number provided by the student should be consistent.
  • The students may fill only one choice and it is advised that the students fill their choice carefully.
  • Once the student has filled in his/her choice, the choice may not be changed. All applications for change of choice will be dealt by the Head Warden separately.
